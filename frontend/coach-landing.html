FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free to register as a coach?

Yes! Creating your profile and listing your services on MoCoach is completely free. You only pay a small commission when you successfully book a session through the platform.

What documents do I need to verify my profile?

A valid government-issued ID and relevant coaching qualifications or certifications. Send them to kyc@mocoach.mu after registration.

How do clients find me?

Clients browse by sport, location, and price range. Your profile appears in relevant searches, making it easy for potential clients in your area to discover you.

Can I edit my profile after registering?

Absolutely. You can update your bio, photos, pricing, and specialties anytime from your coach profile page.
