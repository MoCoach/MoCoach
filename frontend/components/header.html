About One to One

One to One represents Mauritius' first dedicated initiative to centralize and secure the sports, health, and wellness ecosystem. For years, booking qualified local sports expertise has been fragmented across various social networks.

By offering unified profiles, central calendar checks, and client reviews, we help correct transparency imbalances. One to One elevates vetted professional wellness coaches on the island while protecting clients.
