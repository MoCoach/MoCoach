About MoCoach

MoCoach represents Mauritius' first dedicated initiative to centralize and secure the sports, health, and wellness ecosystem. For years, booking qualified local sports expertise has been fragmented across various social networks, leaving both local residents and visiting tourists struggling with timing sync and verification.

By offering unified profiles, central calendar checks, and client reviews, we help correct transparency imbalances. MoCoach elevates verified professional wellness coaches on the island while protecting clients seeking stable wellness habits.
